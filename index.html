About

Pozdravljeni na moji spletni strani. Sem Bor Križaj, obiskujem 4. letnik srednje šole za Računalništvo v Novi Gorici. Z računalništvom sem se začel ukvarjati že v osnovni šoli in od takrat svoje znanje uspešno nadgrajujem. V prostem času se ukvarjam z glasbeno produkcijo ter se učim novih tehnik snemanja ter novih inštrumentov. Znam igrati bobne in ostala tolkala, kitaro, klavir ter se učim še harmoniko in trobento. Sodelujem tudi v večih orkestrih in glasbenih skupinah. Rad pa tudi ustvarjam sam.

Welcome to my website. I am Bor Križaj, I am attending the 4th year of high school for Computer Science in Nova Gorica. I started learning about computers and programming in primary school and I have been successfully upgrading my knowledge ever since. In my spare time I am involved in music production and learning new recording techniques and new instruments. I can play drums and other percussion, guitar, piano and I also learn accordion and trumpet. I also participate in several orchestras and music groups. But I also like to create on my own.
